# 11 -- Legal, Privacy & Analytics

## Intent

A production site needs proper legal coverage and usage tracking. Since we're based in California and handle webcam-based head-pose features, privacy is especially important to get right.

---

## Terms of Service

**Route:** `/terms` (`src/components/TermsPage.tsx`)

Key sections:
- **AI-generated content disclaimer:** Content generated by Claude is for informational/exploratory purposes, no accuracy guarantee
- **Camera/head-pose features:** Explicit consent required, all processing client-side, no video transmitted
- **Intellectual property:** Design, code, AI pipeline, spatial methods protected
- **Prohibited uses:** No scraping, data-mining, harassment, unauthorized access
- **Governing law:** State of California
- **Limitation of liability:** To fullest extent permitted by California law

## Privacy Policy

**Route:** `/privacy` (`src/components/PrivacyPage.tsx`)

**CCPA compliant** — covers California Consumer Privacy Act requirements:

### Data collected
- User-submitted content (posts, replies, votes)
- Basic usage data (pages, features used)
- Device/browser info, IP address

### Camera data (critical)
- **All video processing happens entirely on-device** via MediaPipe
- No video frames, images, or facial data ever sent to servers
- No video recorded or stored
- Only derived yaw/pitch values used, stored only in browser memory
- GazeLearner calibration data stays in browser session
- Explicit consent required, revocable at any time

### CCPA rights
- Right to know, delete, opt-out of sale (we don't sell data), non-discrimination

### Third-party services
- Anthropic (Claude API) — post content sent for AI analysis
- Google MediaPipe — model loaded from CDN, runs locally
- Google Cloud / Firebase — hosting and analytics
- MongoDB — user posts and votes storage
